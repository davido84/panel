Payment information

We use Square payment systems to send you an invoice for the panel tuition. Square payments offer several ways to pay an invoice: with your credit or debit card, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Panel Information

The panels will be conducted using Zoom Video Conferencing software. We will send you the meeting invite, which includes all details needed to join the meeting via ZOOM, after we receive payment. The ZOOM software is free to download and used for all participants of the panel.

Here is the link to download Zoom. Please remember, you only need to sign up for the Basic Plan (which is 100% free).

Personal Conduct Information

When you participate in a panel, the following personal conduct rules apply. Note that we cannot make exceptions to these rules.
  • You must log in to the meeting no later than the published start time
  • You must log in with a computer or cell phone that has a video camera
  • You must be present for the entire duration of the class
  • The camera must be on for the duration of the class

After you have attended a panel

After you have attended the panel, we will send an electronic copy of proof of attendance to you. Note that it is your responsibility to provide a copy of your form to the court and/or your attorney.
